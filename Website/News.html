Latest News

International Conference on Digital Humanities

Posted on October 15, 2024

We are excited to announce that the Digital Humanities Lab at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad will be hosting an International Conference on Digital Humanities in March 2025. The event will bring together scholars, researchers, and students to discuss cutting-edge research and innovations in the field. Stay tuned for the official call for papers and registration details!

New Collaboration with Global South DH Consortium

Posted on September 28, 2024

The Digital Humanities Lab is proud to announce a new collaboration with the Global South DH Consortium. This partnership aims to enhance research in Digital Humanities with a focus on South Asian narratives, postcolonial studies, and minimal computing practices. Joint workshops and events will be scheduled in the upcoming months.

Launch of Student-Led Digital Archive Project

Posted on September 10, 2024

Our MA students have successfully launched a new digital archive project, which aims to preserve local histories through digitized interviews, artifacts, and documents. The project emphasizes the importance of digital preservation in the humanities and will be showcased at the upcoming DH Expo in November.
